About

I'm Jake Dillon, a 29-year-old former special ed teacher and singer songwriter from Philly, now living in Bucks County. I blend folk, country, and indie pop into a sound that's honest and down to earth. I play drums, piano, and guitar on my tracks and write almost every day, drawing inspiration from relationships, heartbreak, and my faith journey. Creating music people can truly connect with is my passion.
